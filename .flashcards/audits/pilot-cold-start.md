## Rejected dependency leaks

- Removed velocity, acceleration, force, energy, momentum, and field from the
  pilot because their mechanics meanings belong to later chapters.
- Removed the free-fall dimensional-analysis problem because it required
  gravitational acceleration before kinematics and interactions were taught.
- Removed abstract scientific-method terminology that did not enable the next
  mechanics decision.
- Introduced each SI quantity–unit mapping on a front before scheduling cloze
  retrieval of that mapping.
